# Hats

PublicHaus uses the [Hats Protocol](https://www.hatsprotocol.xyz/) for our organizational graph, roles, agents, and permissions.

[Hats Tree](https://app.hatsprotocol.xyz/trees/10/24?compact=true&hatId=24.3.2)

Top Hat corresponds to the [Hats Tree Controller](https://app.safe.global/oeth:0xb1027c597e83009aadfc8e0b24db6ba31b1e3c6d/balances) Safe attached to the DAO.
